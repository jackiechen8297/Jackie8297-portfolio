Executive Summary

Healthier foods such as fresh produce have always been on the expensive side. As a result of this, this creates a food insecurity issue among the people who cannot access healthier food options due to lack of affordability. In addition to the existing food insecurity, the high cost of living in New York City makes fresh produce more difficult to access.The objective of this report is to document the process of making a hydroponic kratky system that would make healthier food options more accessible via food pantries and farmers markets.

Project Overview

Background:Growing up in New York City, the cost of living can be very relentless regardless of socio-economic status.. Due to the high cost of living, people have to resort to cheaper food options such as fast food and non-perishable canned foods to get by. As a result, many of those people will be lacking in the nutrients that vegetables provide. The produce item sections in the supermarkets are often high in cost to the point where people can’t afford them. My main intent is to grow some kale microgreens that can be accessed by people who were not able to afford those healthier food options.

What is the problem?:The high cost of fresh produce in New York City, coupled with the lack of affordability for healthier food options, has led to a food insecurity issue. In addition, the high cost of living has made it difficult for people to access fresh produce options.

Factors considered: The growing conditions for this system should be taken into account the most. Making sure the water pH and EC levels are within range that allows for optimal growth. The growing Growth medium content is also optional since microgreens aren’t dependent on growing mediums for overall growth. Water must also never make contact with the electricity due to safety concerns.

Recommendations/Suggested Actions: For water, it is highly suggested to use filtered water for sanitation purposes and ensure the pH level to be around 5.5-6.5 with the use of the pH meter. As for safety, make sure that the growing bins are always evenly leveled to make sure the water doesn’t spill and make contact with the electrical components. The potential water leakage can damage the LED lights or cause the entire system to malfunction.

Rationale for Recommendations/Suggested Actions: In order to fufill the recommendations, prior knowledge of the hydroponic systems and the smaller components of the systems should be taught and introduced before being able to build and sustain a hydroponic system. A basic understanding of water pH/EC is also required in order to maintain the livelihood of the hydroponic system.

Solution

Despite me being very enthusiastic about starting the wick system, I decided to go in another direction. I ended up choosing the kratky system method due to its low maintenance, cost-effectiveness and high efficiency.
